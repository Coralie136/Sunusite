JotLoader was originally developed for Joomla 1.0.x framework based on doQment component, ver.1.0.0 beta, published by pimpmyjoomla.com in May 2007 under GNU/GPL licence. JotLoader, ver.2 is fully reworked solution for Joomla 1.5.x with many new function and features. Here are new commenting functions of bugs, changed operation for bugs maintenance, diverse assignments of download files to menu as well as functions for multiple operations with files and categories.

JotLoader name:
In Websters dictionary has the noun "jot" following meaning :
1. A brief (and hurriedly handwritten) note.
2. A small but appreciable amount.
JotLoader name shall express the ability of downloader component to work with short comments (notifications) to each download link.
